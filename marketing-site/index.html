How It Works

Three steps. Zero phone calls.

Your customer gets an answer in minutes. You get a qualified lead with photos, contact info, and the estimate they saw.

1

Customer uploads photos

They visit your branded estimate page, fill in their contact info, and upload 1-3 photos of the tree. Takes under 2 minutes on any phone.

2

AI analyzes and estimates

Our AI identifies the tree species, size, condition, and complexity — then generates a realistic price range based on your local market rates.

3

Both sides get notified

You get an email (and SMS on Pro) with their contact info and the estimate shown. They get a confirmation with your contact info and a prompt to book.
